## 5. Client responsibilities

The Client will:

- Confirm it owns or has permission to use all supplied materials.
- Provide one consolidated set of feedback per revision round.
- Review deliverables and respond within `[3]` business days.

## 6. Revisions and change requests

The project includes `[NUMBER]` revision rounds. A revision adjusts work within the accepted direction and scope; it is not a new concept, added page or new feature.

Out-of-scope requests require written approval of added price and schedule before work begins.

## 7. Acceptance and correction period

The Client accepts a milestone by written approval, public use or failure to report a material agreed-scope defect within `[5]` business days of delivery.

For `[14]` calendar days after launch, Iluminity will correct reproducible defects that prevent agreed functionality. This excludes new requests, client modifications, third-party changes, unsupported environments and approved content errors.

## 8. Intellectual property

After full payment, the Client receives the rights stated in Exhibit A to final custom deliverables.

Iluminity retains all rights to pre-existing tools, reusable code, systems, frameworks, processes, know-how and unused concepts. Iluminity may display publicly launched work in its portfolio unless Exhibit A says otherwise.

## 9. Third-party services

Domains, hosting, fonts, stock media, plugins, payment processors, booking tools, email services and other third-party products are governed by their providers. The Client is responsible for third-party fees unless Exhibit A says otherwise.

Iluminity is not responsible for third-party outages, account suspensions, pricing changes or policy changes.

## 10. No result guarantee

Iluminity does not guarantee sales, revenue, lead volume, search ranking, platform approval or any specific business result. Iluminity agrees to provide the stated deliverables using reasonable professional care.

## 11. Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, special or consequential damages or lost profits.

Iluminity’s aggregate liability relating to this project will not exceed the amount actually paid to Iluminity under this Agreement. Limitations apply only to the extent legally permitted.

If the Client cancels for convenience, completed work and committed scheduling remain payable. Iluminity will provide paid-for completed deliverables after all outstanding amounts are resolved.
